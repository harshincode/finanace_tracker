Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and learn how to use the Personal Finance Tracker effectively.

How do I add a new transaction?

To add a new transaction, navigate to the 'Dashboard' section and click on the '+ Add Transaction' button. This will take you to a form where you can enter the details of your income or expense.

How can I set a new financial goal?

Go to the 'Goals' section and click the '+ Add Goal' button. You can then specify the goal title, target amount, category, and target date.

Where can I see a breakdown of my spending?

The 'Dashboard' provides a summary of your monthly income and expenses. For a detailed list of all your transactions, visit the 'Transactions' section.
